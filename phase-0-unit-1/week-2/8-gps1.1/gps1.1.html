Peer Pairing

Driver

Aside from physically writing the code throughout the session, the driver collaborates with the navigator to provide feedback regarding the approach in code to be written.

Navigator

The navigator serves as the primary strategist and designer to steer the driver in the right direction when writing the code.

Pairing Dynamics

The driver and navigator pair should work as a team to both compromise on the best approach into completing the task at hand and filling each others knowledge gaps, since each individual brings diverse knowledge, experience and skillsets to the pair.
